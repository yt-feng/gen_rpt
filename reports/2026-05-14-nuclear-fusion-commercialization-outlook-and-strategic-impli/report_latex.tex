\textcolor{BOBlue}{\bfseries 04} & {\footnotesize Key findings: fusion could achieve cost parity with renewables by 2050 under optimistic scenarios, but faces risks from regulatory gaps and tritium scarcity.} \\[4pt]
\textcolor{BOBlue}{\bfseries 05} & {\footnotesize Strategic implications include potential disruption of fossil fuel markets, new geopolitical dependencies, and a need for early investment in supply chains and regulatory frameworks.} \\[4pt]
